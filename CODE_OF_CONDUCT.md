# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We aim to create a welcoming, safe, and inclusive environment for everyone.  
All participants are expected to treat others with respect, regardless of race, ethnicity, gender, age, ability, sexual orientation, religion, socio-economic status, or any other characteristic.

We commit to fostering a community where everyone can contribute, learn, and collaborate in good faith.

---

## Expected Behaviors

Participants should strive to:

- Be **kind, respectful, and professional** in all interactions
- Respect **different viewpoints and experiences**
- Give and accept **constructive feedback** gracefully
- Take responsibility for their actions and contributions
- Help maintain a **supportive and inclusive community**

---

## Unacceptable Behaviors

The following are not allowed:

- Harassment, intimidation, or unwanted attention
- Insults, personal attacks, or demeaning comments
- Discrimination or stereotyping
- Sharing private information without permission
- Threats or acts of violence
- Any behavior that undermines community safety or well-being

Other actions that violate the spirit of the community, such as impersonation, misleading communication, or inappropriate promotion, are also prohibited.

---

## Reporting

If you experience or witness a violation, report it to a project maintainer or admin confidentially.  
Reports will be taken seriously, investigated thoroughly, and handled with respect for safety and privacy.

---

## Enforcement

Violations may result in actions including:

1. **Warning** – private notification and request to correct behavior
2. **Temporary Restriction** – limited access to project spaces for reflection
3. **Temporary Suspension** – removal from project spaces for a defined period
4. **Permanent Ban** – removal from all project spaces if harm is severe or repeated

Actions are applied based on the severity of the violation and may skip steps as necessary.

---
